Alycia Rockey

Alycia needed a visual identity to tie her personal brand, social channels, and portfolio together. I designed a circular monogram and printed it glossy onto matte cards which showcased her brand colors. The colors give off a retro, tropical feel, which adds a bit of whimsy and personality where so many photo identities are stark black and white.
