Century Center

Century Center is a two-building office complex with retail, food, and much more in the heart of Alexandria, Virginia. Cushman & Wakefield of Washington, D.C., the commercial representative for the property came to me for a website to showcase the property as a whole as well as provide specific information for prospective tenants.

We worked together to have beautiful imagery and easy interaction with floor plans and maps.
