Southern Railway Building

This is a site to promote the Southern Railway Building and its spaces. The building is a large office building in the heart of McPherson Square in Washington D.C.

The project included a custom “Build Your Space” feature which allows you to draw on top of floor plans in multiple colors to envision planning an office layout. You can then export this to your computer.
