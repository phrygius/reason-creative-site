Wedding Invitation

This project was surprisingly difficult because it's always hard to be your own client. Something about the Cobbler's children…

Alycia and I knew we wanted to be very floral with our motif so we bought a lot of flowers from a local florist, and set up a home photo shoot with some scrap wood as a background. The main goal of the project was to have a very elegant invitation that spoke to the formal nature of the event. This was also important to stress that even though it took place on October 31, it was not going to be a costume party.
